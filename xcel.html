Xcel / Advanced

A bridge between recreational and competitive gymnastics

Xcel Program Overview

The Xcel program is a test or invitation only advanced pathway designed for dedicated gymnasts who are ready to push beyond recreational training. Athletes gain structured progressions, richer skill development, and — at the competitive level — the opportunity to compete at invitationals across Canada and the United States.

Who Is Xcel For?

Xcel sits between our recreational stream and our full Ontario Competitive Program. It is ideal for athletes who demonstrate strong potential and commitment but may not yet be ready for — or interested in — the demands of the full OCP schedule. The program builds skill, strength, and a competitive mindset in a structured yet flexible environment.

Younger athletes often begin in Pre-Xcel before progressing to Competitive Xcel or moving into the Ontario Competitive Program. All Xcel programs are by test or invitation only following a free assessment with our coaching staff.

Xcel / Advanced Programs

The Xcel/Advanced stream has two tracks. Pre-Xcel / Showcase Xcel is the entry point for athletes ages 6+; Competitive Xcel is for those ready to train and compete at a higher level. All tracks are by test or invitation only.

Pre-Xcel / Showcase Xcel

Ages 6+ By test or invitation only

Pre-Xcel and Showcase Xcel introduce athletes to the advanced training environment and structured skill progressions of the Xcel program. Athletes develop the strength, technique, and confidence needed to perform in local showcase events and prepare for the Competitive Xcel or Ontario Competitive Program streams.

Trains 3.5–7 hours per week
Participates in local showcase events
Natural progression to Competitive Xcel or the Ontario Competitive Program (OCP)
ASF Goal To provide a structured and encouraging stepping stone for young athletes with advanced potential — developing foundational skills, physical conditioning, and a love for the sport in preparation for higher-level programs.

Competitive Xcel

Ages 8+ By test or invitation only

Competitive Xcel is a full competitive stream for athletes ready to train at high intensity and represent ASF at invitational competitions across Canada and the United States. Athletes progress through four levels — Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum — each with increasingly demanding skill requirements.

Trains 7–12 hours per week
Competes at invitational competitions across Canada and the United States
ASF Goal To challenge committed athletes within a structured competitive framework that develops strength, technical mastery, mental resilience, and a strong team identity — all while building a passion for the sport that lasts a lifetime.

How to Join

All Xcel programs are by test or invitation only. We offer a free assessment for prospective athletes so our coaches can determine the best program fit.

Free Assessment

  1. Submit your assessment request using the link below. Include your child’s age, current gymnastics experience, and any programs they have previously trained in.
  2. Our coaching staff will review your inquiry and reach out to schedule an assessment session at a time that works for you.
  3. Your child attends an on-floor assessment where our coaches evaluate current skill level, strength, and overall gymnastics readiness.
  4. If selected, your coach will guide you through next steps for enrolment, training schedules, and program fees.

Assessments are held throughout the year. No advanced gymnastics background is required for Pre-Xcel — just enthusiasm and commitment.
