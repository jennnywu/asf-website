Our National-level coaching staff works with athletes across every program level — from Pre-Competitive through High Performance — providing consistent, world-class technical instruction from day one.

About Our Competitive Team

Through our dynamic gymnastics program and the individualization of each athlete, our gymnasts develop life skills such as self-discipline, independence, self-esteem, and the ability to cope with both success and challenge — all in a positive and constructive environment.

A team atmosphere is highly encouraged at ASF. Our coaching staff monitors all programs from Pre-Competitive through High Performance, ensuring every athlete receives the same technical foundation throughout the levels. Proper gymnastics development from beginner to elite is at the core of everything we do.

All competitive programs are by test or invitation only. Prospective athletes are invited to a free assessment to evaluate readiness for the program.

Competitive Programs

Our competitive stream includes three progressively advanced programs. All are 12-month commitments with consistent training schedules from September through June, with optional summer hours in July and August.

Pre-Competitive Program

Girls Ages 5–7 By test or invitation only

The Pre-Competitive Program prepares young athletes physically, technically, and mentally for entry into the competitive gymnastics stream. Athletes are introduced to competition in a fun, friendly, and team-oriented environment.

Trains 4–12 hours per week depending on selected group
Competes in Gymnastics Ontario ODP competitions testing strength, flexibility, endurance, and speed
ASF Goal To help young athletes develop strong gymnastics fundamentals — strength, flexibility, and coordination — in a fun and supportive environment that prepares them for future success in the competitive program and the sport as a whole.

Ontario Competitive Program (Levels 1–9)

Girls Ages 7+ By test or invitation only

Athletes in the Ontario Competitive Program (OCP) train and compete through Gymnastics Ontario’s official competitive levels. This is a 12-month commitment with consistent training from September through June. Optional summer hours are available in July and August.

Trains 12–20+ hours per week based on level — this is a 12-month program
Levels 1–2 compete in invitational competitions only
Levels 3–9 compete at Provincial qualifying competitions with the opportunity to advance to the Ontario Championships, as well as invitational competitions across Canada and the United States
Athletes in Level 7+ have the opportunity to represent Team Ontario at Eastern and National Canadian Championships
ASF Goal All ASF competitive athletes are given every opportunity to reach their full potential. These athletes not only reach their goals in their chosen sport, but also acquire the life skills along the way that prepare them to become strong, independent young women.

National (Level 10+) / High Performance

Girls Ages 11+ By test or invitation only

The National Program is for athletes who have developed the skills to compete at a National and International level. This program is demanding while balancing both the physical and mental aspects of elite athletic development.

Trains 20–30 hours per week — 12-month program
Competes for the opportunity to represent Ontario and/or Canada nationally and internationally
Competitions include:
  • · Elite Canada
  • · Canadian National Championships
  • · Pan American Games
  • · Commonwealth Games
  • · World Cup events
  • · Olympic Games
ASF is proud to have had athletes represent us at every event listed above.
University Scholarships — including NCAA D1 scholarships — are available to ASF competitive athletes. Our athletes have attended UCLA, Stanford, University of Arizona, University of Michigan, UC Berkeley, and University of Hawaii.
ASF Goal All ASF competitive athletes are given every opportunity to reach their full potential. These athletes not only reach their goals in their chosen sport, but also acquire the life skills along the way that prepare them to become strong, independent young women.

How to Join Our Team

All competitive programs are by test or invitation only. We offer free assessments for prospective athletes — this is your first step toward joining the ASF competitive family.

Free Assessment

  1. Submit your assessment request using the form below. Include your child’s age, current gymnastics experience, and any programs they’ve previously trained in.
  2. Our coaching staff will review your inquiry and contact you to schedule an assessment session.
  3. Your child attends a free on-floor assessment with our coaches to evaluate their current skills and discuss the best program fit.
  4. If selected, your coach will walk you through next steps for enrolment, training schedules, and fees.

Assessments are held throughout the year, with additional sessions in May and June for the upcoming season. No gymnastics background is required for the Pre-Competitive program.
