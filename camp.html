Preparing for Camp

Everything you need to know before your first day. Read through before camp starts — it makes drop-off smoother for everyone!

Drop-Off

  • Doors open at 8:50am — please arrive between 8:50 and 9:00am.
  • Late arrivals are admitted only when a coach can escort them in without disrupting the group.
  • Parents and caregivers must sign campers in at the door with coaching staff.
  • If your child will be absent, please give us advance notice.

Pick-Up

  • Pick-up is at 4:00pm (5:30pm for extended care).
  • Coaches will sign campers out. Bring photo ID if staff doesn't know you.
  • Only authorized individuals listed on file may pick up a child.

What to Bring

  • 2 nut-free snacks (morning & afternoon).
  • A substantial lunch — no fridge or microwave available.
  • Full water bottle labeled with your child's name.
  • Form-fitting clothing: shorts, t-shirt, or gymnastics leotard.
  • No skirts, dresses, or hoodies.
  • Long hair pulled back; no necklaces or hanging jewelry.
  • Campers go barefoot in the gym.

Gym Rules & Safety

  • Equipment use requires coach supervision at all times.
  • Remove outdoor shoes in the front lobby before the gym.
  • Only athletes and staff in gym areas — no parent viewing during camp.
  • Food and drinks in designated areas only.
  • Peanut & nut-free facility — no products containing nuts.

Communications

  • Coaches are not available during camp hours.
  • Email questions are answered by the head coach as soon as possible.
  • Please avoid entering the gym during camp operations.

Health & Safety

  • If your child is sick or unwell, please keep them home and follow medical guidance.
  • Health policies may be updated in line with provincial and public health regulations.
